Every active SFMTA parking meter, colored by its current policy. Sources: Parking Meters · Meter Policies (DataSF; policies are a daily snapshot of current schedules). About a quarter of active meters — multi-space pay stations and most motorcycle meters — publish no schedule and show as “no data.”
